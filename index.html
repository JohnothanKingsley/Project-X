Eggy in the Basket

Follow these steps carefully and you will seduce a king

Steps:

  1. Heat REAL butter in a pan just before cooking point.
  2. While the butter melts, cut a circle in the middle of the bread, about the size of a cup.
  3. Place the bread in the pan and break an egg into the open space.
  4. Drip some of the butter onto the egg and add some Cyan pepper for flavour.
  5. Place the cut out bread over it.
  6. Flip the basket and make sure it seals.
  7. Serve with your favourite condiment.
  8. Savor the pure ecstasy on the face of your guest.
